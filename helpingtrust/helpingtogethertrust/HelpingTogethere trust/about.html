Our team

Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a brighter future for orphaned children. Our mission is to create a safe and loving environment where these children can thrive and grow. With a focus on education, healthcare, and emotional support, we aim to empower every child to reach their full potential.
Founded in 2009, this trust is started by two friends they have been serving our community for years. In 2018 they created a group where they circulated about the trust interested people are added to the group.
In 2021 August 23 their Orphanage got registered by Andhra Pradesh Govt.
There are 32 trust group members till now.
